I am a Professor of Economics at the University of Manitoba, based in Winnipeg Canada, with a PhD in economics from <a href="http://economics.wustl.edu/">Washington University</a>. I have been a visitor at the <a href="http://www.bcb.gov.br/?english">Central Bank of Brazil</a>, <a href="http://economics.uwo.ca/">University of Western Ontario</a>,  <a href="https://neu.edu.vn/">National Economics University </a>(Hanoi), and <a href="https://www.dhbw.de/startseite.html">Duale Hochschule Baden-Württemberg</a>. Prior to academia I contributed to public policy through roles in Canada's <a href="https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/corporate/job-opportunities/advanced-policy-analyst-program.html">Accelerated Economist Program</a><a href="http://apap.gc.ca/home"> </a>(now the Advanced Policy Analyst Program) and <a href="https://www.canada.ca/en/department-finance.html">Department of Finance Canada</a>.

My research spans macroeconomics, development, institutions, conflict, and defence economics, and my work has appeared recently in journals such as the <i>Southern Economic Journal</i>, <i>Economic Modelling</i>, <i>Defence and Peace Economics</i>, and the <i>Journal of Economics, Race, and Policy</i>.

I currently serve on the executive of the <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/cdesg/">Canadian Development Economics Study Group </a>, co-organize the annual <a href="https://www.defenceandsecurityeconomicsworkshop.ca/">Defence and Security Economics Workshop</a>, and sit on the editorial boards of <a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="http://www.tandfonline.com/toc/gdpe20/current" target="_blank">Defence and Peace Economics</a> and the <a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="http://www.springer.com/economics/policy/journal/41996" target="_blank">Journal of Economics, Race, and Policy</a>.
