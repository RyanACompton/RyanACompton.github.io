I am a Professor of Economics at the University of Manitoba.

My research focuses on development economics, institutions, conflict, macroeconomics, and defence economics, with applications spanning economic growth, migration, labour markets, and public policy.

I serve on the executive of the Canadian Development Economics Study Group and the Defence and Security Economics Workshop.
