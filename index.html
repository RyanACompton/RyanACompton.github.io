I am a Professor of Economics at the University of Manitoba, with a PhD in economics from Washington University.

My research examines how institutional structures, economic policy, and conflict shape economic outcomes across countries and populations.

I currently serve on the executives of the Canadian Development Economics Study Group , the Defence and Security Economics Workshop, and the editorial boards of Defence and Peace Economics and the Journal of Economics, Race, and Policy.
